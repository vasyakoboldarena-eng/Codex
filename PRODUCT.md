# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Decision makers and teams inside large companies: executives, marketing, sales, HR, learning, strategy, and internal communications. They need high-stakes presentations that are clear, polished, and ready for leadership, clients, investors, or conferences.

## Product Purpose

This one-page personal site presents Kim Voronin as a presentation designer and strategist for corporate presentations. Success means a visitor understands within five seconds that Kim can quickly turn complex business material into premium, structured, persuasive slides for large-company contexts.

## Brand Personality

Premium, fast, reliable. The voice is calm, precise, senior, and business-literate. It should feel like a specialist who can speak with top managers at eye level.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI landing pages, beige minimalism, corporate-blue templates, cyberpunk styling, fake testimonials, fake client logos, inflated claims, gradient text, decorative glassmorphism, and repetitive icon-card grids.

## Design Principles

- Lead with business confidence, not decorative design language.
- Show that the work is strategic: structure, meaning, and executive context matter as much as visual polish.
- Use restraint with one sharp accent so the page feels like a premium document.
- Make the portfolio area honest: use placeholders until real PDFs and examples are provided.
- Keep the call to action direct and low-friction: leave a request, describe the task, get the next step.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, clear hierarchy, keyboard-friendly form controls, responsive layout, and reduced-motion support. Avoid relying on color alone for meaning.
